About Me

I am a physcian specializing in the care of chronic wounds. I treat patients with all types of chronic skin damage, including burns, pressure sores, diabetic ulcers, and non-healing surgical wounds.

I was a paramedic prior to Medical School.

I studied wound care extensively during my residency training, including plastic surgery, vascular surgery, orthopedic surgery, and trauma surgery.

I have been in the medical field for over 30 years.

I spend my spare time working with Scouting America, teaching aquatics, including swimming, canoeing, kayaking, and swiftwater rescue.
