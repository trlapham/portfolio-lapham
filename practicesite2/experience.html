Wound Care Physician

Practice Limited to Medical and Surgical Wound Care

Practice focus is wound and skin care, trained in General Surgery.

Training and work history includes treatment of pressure ulcers, vascular wounds, diabetic wounds, traumatic wounds, burn wounds, complicated post-operative wounds, neoplastic wounds, radiation-induced wounds, and lymphedematous wounds.

Special interests include wounds in the geriatric population, diabetic wounds, vascular wounds, and wounds complicated by morbid obesity and autoimmune disease.

Surgical wound care practice with experience in complex wound reconstruction.

Single-physician practice, with direct patient contact to physician throughout the entire care process.

Formerly Medical Director Inova Loudoun Wound Center

PATCH, Leesburg, VA — Inova Loudoun Hospital has relocated and expanded their Wound Healing Center to the Inova Loudoun Medical Campus – Lansdowne, 44055 Riverside Parkway, Suite 118. The Center’s space is now double in size and occupies approximately 3,000 square-feet of space consisting of six private treatment bays and a conference room.

The Center provides state-of-the-art advanced wound care for people of all ages. The team of physicians, including the only board certified wound specialist physician in Loudoun County, and nurses provide care that is customized for people with chronic non-healing wounds from any cause. The Inova Wound Healing Center – Loudoun has been treating patients for over eight years and is one of a kind in Loudoun County.

“Our new location gives us better visibility and a greater opportunity to serve our community by seeing more patients,” commented Medical Director Seemi Andrabi, MD, Inova Wound Healing Center – Loudoun.
