A Brief Description of My Training and Accomplishments
Full Details On My Awesome Website TimothyLapham.com.

Practice focus is wound and skin care, trained in General Surgery. Training and work history includes treatment of pressure ulcers, vascular wounds, diabetic wounds, traumatic wounds, burn wounds, complicated post-operative wounds, neoplastic wounds, radiation-induced wounds, and lymphedematous wounds. Special interests include wounds in the geriatric population, diabetic wounds, vascular wounds, and wounds complicated by morbid obesity and autoimmune disease. Current practice limited to facility patient and in-home care.
