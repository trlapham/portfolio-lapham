Work Experience

Wound Care Physician

Practice focus is wound and skin care, trained in General Surgery. Training and work history includes treatment of pressure ulcers, vascular wounds, diabetic wounds, traumatic wounds, burn wounds, complicated post-operative wounds, neoplastic wounds, radiation-induced wounds, and lymphedematous wounds. Special interests include wounds in the geriatric population, diabetic wounds, vascular wounds, and wounds complicated by morbid obesity and autoimmune disease.

Responsibilities

  • Diagnose acute and chronic diseases of the skin
  • Treat wound patients
  • Prescribe medicationas need for skin infection
  • Prevent skin damage
